Kenneth is a prominent American economist and Professor of Economics at #Harvard . He previously served as Chief Economist and Director of Research at the International Monetary Fund (IMF) from 2001 to 2003. Rogoff is widely recognized for his work on international finance, central bank independence, and financial crises. He is also an International Grandmaster of chess.
