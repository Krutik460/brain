Adam Grant is an organizational psychologist, professor, and author known for his work on motivation, leadership, and organizational behavior. He is the host of the popular TED podcast "WorkLife" and has written several best-selling books on topics such as creativity, generosity, and success. Grant is a professor at the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania and has been recognized as one of the world's most influential management thinkers.
