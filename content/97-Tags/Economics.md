This page is a curated repository of my learnings and resources related to economics, organized into key fields for quick reference. My journey starts with microeconomics and econometrics, and will expand from there.

## Core Fields of Economics

- #Microeconomics - Understanding the world through the lens of incentives, trade-offs, and individual decision-making.
- #Econometrics - The tools and techniques for using data to answer causal questions and measure economic relationships.
- #Macroeconomics - Exploring the broad economic factors that influence national and global economies, like inflation, growth, and unemployment.
- #BehavioralEconomics - Learning how psychological factors influence economic decisions and market outcomes.
- #GameTheory - Exploring the strategic interactions among rational decision-makers in economics.
- #EconomicHistory - Diving into the historical events and trends that have shaped modern economies.
