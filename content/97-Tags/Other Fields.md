## Philosophy
- #Epistemology - Discover information regarding the study of knowledge—its nature, sources, limits, and validity. It explores questions like: _What is knowledge? How do we know what we know?_
- #Metaphysics - Discover information regarding the study of reality and existence. It deals with fundamental questions like: _What is real? What is the nature of being, time, space, and causality?_
- #Logic - Discover information regarding the study of reasoning and argumentation. It focuses on the rules and principles that guide valid inference and sound thinking.
- #Axiology - Discover information regarding the study of value. It further divides the study into first **ethics** where we explores moral values—_what is right or wrong, good or bad_, and second **aesthetics** where we examines beauty, art, and taste—_what is pleasing or meaningful in experience_.
- #PoliticalPhilosophy - Discover information regarding the study of government, justice, rights, and the role of individuals in society. It asks questions like: _What makes a government legitimate? What is freedom or equality?_
